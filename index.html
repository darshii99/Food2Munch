Knowing a bit more about coffee...

Independent

These coffee shop are local business sourcing ingredients (including coffee beans!) from the local area.
Visiting these coffee shops is a great way to support local businesses and learn more about produce around the area. The coffee served in these coffee shops are normally sourced from nearby roasteries (e.g Clifton Roasters).

Specialty

Coffee served here are normally sourced from a single-origin beans, meaning that ALL the beans are from the same plantation! This means that the taste of the coffee will be very unique to the specific climate those beans grew in.
Baristas in these shops are very knowledgeable about the process of the coffee from bean to cup so don't be shy to ask them about it!
